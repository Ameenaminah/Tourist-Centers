The most popular tourist destination in Nigeria

Obudu Mountain Resort is a ranch and resort in Cross River State developed in 1951 by M. McCaughley, a Scot who first explored the mountain ranges in 1949. Formerly known as the Obudu Cattle Ranch, Obudu Mountain Resort has in recent times recorded visits from both Nigerian and international tourists because of the development of the facilities by Cross-River State Government, which has turned the ranch into a well-known holiday and tourist resort centre in Niger.
